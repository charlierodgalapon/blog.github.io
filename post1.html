"You can't change the past but you can learn from it."

A quote from Alice through the looking glass that teaches us to think first because it affects the future.

Posted by Cess Runas November 3, 2016

The past is history. Cherish it, relish it, but don’t live in it. Otherwise, you will be missing out on what is in front of you now. Always live in the present.

You can’t change what has happened, only what has yet to come. Don’t waste your future time trying to re-write the past. Instead, spend your hard work on how to make your future better.

Don’t fall into the nostalgia trap that things were always better in the past. Things are different now. What worked once up a time, may not be relevant to the way things work today.
